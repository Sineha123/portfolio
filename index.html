Biography & Education

I am currently an Associate Professor of Data and Cybersecurity at the College of Computing and Information Technology, University of Doha for Science & Technology (UDST), Doha, Qatar. Recognized among the top 2% of research scientists globally by Stanford University and Elsevier for four consecutive years (2022–2025), I maintain a rigorous balance of Research (60%), Teaching (30%), and Community Service (10%).

I completed my Ph.D. in Information Systems at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) in 2013, with a doctoral thesis titled “Energy Efficient and Low Carbon Enabler Green IT Framework for Data Centers”. Throughout my 14-year academic career, I have served in key roles across Malaysia, UAE, KSA, Pakistan, and Brunei, consistently receiving performance evaluations above 90%.
