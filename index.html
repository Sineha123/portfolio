Biography

Top 2% Research Scientist | Senior Member IEEE

I am currently an Associate Professor of Data and Cybersecurity at the University of Doha for Science & Technology (UDST), Qatar. My primary motivations for employment are teaching, research, and community service. I have maintained balance by demonstrating strong competency in Research (60%), Teaching (30%), and Community Service (10%).

I completed my Ph.D. in Information Systems at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) in 2013. My doctoral thesis was titled “Energy Efficient and Low Carbon Enabler Green IT Framework for Data Centers in Pakistan”, where I proposed an implementation framework to evaluate and deploy energy-efficient cloud data centers. The findings were published in a Tier-1 journal, Renewable & Sustainable Energy Reviews (IF-14.9).

Throughout my 14-year academic career, I have gained international recognition through innovative research, strong leadership, and hands-on teaching in Cybersecurity, Digital Forensics, Blockchain, and Cloud Computing. This is shown by consistent excellence with scores above 90% in faculty performance evaluations over the past eight years at various universities in Malaysia, UAE, KSA, Pakistan, and Brunei.

I have authored over 200 papers in reputable Scopus and SCI-indexed journals. Notably, more than 180 of my publications are in Q-ranked journals with a combined Impact Factor exceeding 400. I am listed among the top 2% of research scientists by Stanford University and Elsevier for 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025.
