Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor of Data and Cybersecurity at the College of Computing and Information Technology, University of Doha for Science & Technology (UDST), Doha, Qatar. My academic journey is driven by a passion for teaching, innovative research, and community service. I have consistently maintained a high standard of performance, balancing Research (60%), Teaching (30%), and Service (10%).

I completed my Ph.D. in Information Systems at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) in 2013[cite: 22]. My doctoral thesis, titled “Energy Efficient and Low Carbon Enabler Green IT Framework for Data Centers”, proposed a groundbreaking framework for evaluating and deploying energy-efficient cloud data centers. The findings were published in the Tier-1 journal, Renewable & Sustainable Energy Reviews[cite: 22, 23].

Throughout my 14-year career, I have held academic positions in Malaysia, UAE, KSA, Pakistan, and Brunei[cite: 25]. My research expertise spans Cybersecurity, Digital Forensics, Blockchain & Distributed Ledger Technology, and Cloud Computing. I have authored over 220 peer-reviewed papers, with more than 180 in Q-ranked journals, achieving a cumulative Impact Factor exceeding 400[cite: 27, 29]. I am also the recipient of multiple research grants and awards, including the "Best Researcher Award" from UDST in 2024[cite: 214].
